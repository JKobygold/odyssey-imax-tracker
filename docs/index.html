Questions people yell into search bars

Why is every The Odyssey IMAX show sold out?

IMAX 70mm is only playing on a handful of screens in the entire country, and The Odyssey is the movie everyone wants to see on them. Shows sell out within minutes of release — what's usually left is the front rows. This page watches the seat maps so you can catch refunds and newly released showtimes.

What counts as a “good seat”?

Anything outside the front third of the auditorium. The front rows at a giant-format screen are technically seats, spiritually a neck injury.

How often does this update?

The tracker polls Regal's seat maps roughly every 15 minutes, prioritizing the next three days of shows. Each showtime row shows when its seats were last checked (hover).

Which theaters show The Odyssey in IMAX 70mm?

Regal's 70mm venues tracked here: REG King of Prussia 4DX & IMAX (King Of Prussia, PA).

Can I track theaters near me?

Yes — this tracker is open source and works for any US zip code and any movie. Clone it, set your zip in config.json, and run it on your own machine. https://github.com/JKobygold/odyssey-imax-tracker
